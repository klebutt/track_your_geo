## 2\. Vision & Conviction

*Why does this product need to exist in the world? What's the insight or belief that makes you think now is the right time?*

- **The big-picture vision** (what does the world look like if this succeeds?): Any business can sign up to Track Your GEO and within minutes see a comprehensive picture of how visible their brand is on generative AI platforms. Track Your GEO monitors the things that matter most to brands \- how they appear, why and how they can change it.   
- **Why now?** (what's changed — technology, behaviour, regulation — that creates the opening?): Search engines are rapidly being displaced by generative AI platforms like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini etc.,. Google is increasingly defaulting to gemini derived search results rather than traditional webpage listings. This matters for brands who need to be visible to their customers or risk declining revenue and retention. If brands don't appear in AI search results \- they don't exist in the eyes of their customers.   
- **Your unique insight** (what do you believe that most people don't?): Existing companies with GEO offerings are primarily service based with a US focus. They act as consultancies charging huge fees to help companies improve their AI relevance often in vague or opaque ways. Track Your GEO is different: A simple, low-cost subscription fee unlocks an easy to understand score and tangible recommendations for improvement. Our proprietary scoring methodology is automated and works around the clock to ensure clients have an accurate picture at all times. The larger GEO tools are plug-ins for Semrush / Ahrefs are plug-ins for much larger SEO tools, other stand-alone tools are EU based. There doesn’t exist a UK-only GEO tracking tool for mid-market firms.

## 3\. Problem Statement

*What specific pain are you solving? Data is ideal, but founder intuition and conversations count — just label which is which.*

- **Who has the problem**: Brand owners. In small firms this is c-suite / MD led. Medium-Large: PR, Comms, SEO, Marketing teams.  
- **What the problem is**: LLMs pull data from a range of sources that are currently unknown to Brands, resulting in an image & narrative that is outside of their control.  
- **How they cope today** (current workaround or alternative):It’s so new, they don’t. They use traditional SEO to boost up the searching rankings, marketing to manage narrative and PR/Comms to manage media exposure… Indirectly affecting GEO results, but no one true score underpinning how effective this is.  
- **Why existing solutions fall short**: They either require you to have larger subscriptions to Semrush/Ahref. Are EU-based (LLM answers change based upon region) or are angled at purely large firms.

*Be ruthlessly specific about your first users — not the eventual mass market. Who are the earliest adopters who will love this enough to tolerate a rough v1?*

- **Early adopter persona** (who are your first 100 users?): Small/Medium sector-specific firms (easier to provide broad LLM responses to industry Q’s i.e.who can help my best manage regulatory questions in legal healthcare). What’s the best Conveyancing firm in London.

- **Key user characteristics** (behaviour, demographics, context of use): tech-savvy, forward thinking on how best to drive new business (can also be sales/CRO/CSO focused)... firms found via LLMs have a much higher conversion ratio (31% greater) than when found via Searching. So LLM appearance drives revenue.

## 5\. Proposed Solution

*Describe what the product does at a high level. Focus on the core value proposition, not implementation details.*

- **Core concept**:TrackYourGEO provides firms with a platform to understand, manage and control their Brand narrative across native LLMs.   
    
- **Key capabilities** (bullet the 3-5 things the product must do):   
  1\) Analyse Brand mentions across industry/sector questions i.e. best CRM for HR professionals   
  2\) Analyze the sentiment of questions about your brand i.e. is Personio a good tool for HR?   
  3\) Present this data in a single score (GEO appearance / sentiment / source list domain authority)  
  4\) Provide options to better your score (business website improvements, Reddit engagement, boosting PR/Comms with high DA media sources)  
    
- **What it explicitly does NOT do** (scope boundaries): We do not provide outsourced SEO/Marketing/Comms activity \- we cannot write blogs, engage in PR. We provide actionable insights for firms to take away and drive.
